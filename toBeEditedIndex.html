About Engrid Hernandez

Growing up, my father was a tech buff who worked in IT and we had a lot of niche gadgets in the house. Because of this exposure innovative technology has always been exciting to me. I remember once he was building himself a PC and I don’t think he had a moment of silence through all of the questions I had for him. I wanted to know why each piece was needed, what would happen without it, if it would need to be replaced someday, and anything else he knew about the piece.

I loved “Part of your World” from the Little Mermaid because I could relate to her admiration for her little understood trinkets. As an adult, “I've got gadgets and gizmos aplenty, I've got whozits and whatzits galore.” This curiosity has stuck with me and I’m excited by learning why things work the way that they do. It feels fantastic being able to say I know something I didn’t know yesterday. I am the upbeat soul telling you that if today feels hard then tomorrow will seem easier. I am ready to learn more from my peers and the direct experience I will gain every day working with your company.
